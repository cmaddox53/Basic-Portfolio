About Me

Chanel Maddox, born in Kansas to Phillip and Vickie Maddox, is a Technical Analyst at Cerner Corporation. As the mother of a 4 year old girl, there is not a day that goes by where she does not hear the name "Mommy" a minimum of 75 times. As a mother, it is her role and responsibility to be the best negotiator possible, janitor, maid, playmate, "ouch detector", and human pillow, who is always available.

Aside from her motherly duties, she plays the role of an Analyst by day, and a web developer by night. Life is pretty hectic, juggling classes, work, a child, and other endless responsibilities, but she is determined to accomplish them all.
